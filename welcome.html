Instructions and Consent

Welcome to this experiment in decision-making!
  • We will ask you to take decisions in several interactive situations.
  • In addition to your participation fee, you can earn a bonus payment.
  • Your bonus payment will depend on:
    • your own decisions; and,
    • on the decisions of another person you are randomly paired with.
  • One of your decisions will be randomly selected at the end to determine your bonus payment.
  • Note that, all of your choice are equally likely to be selected for payment.
By clicking on " I consent" button below, you give your informed consent to the use of your data for research purposes.The data will be stored anonymously, and cannot be traced back to you personally. If you no longer participate, you can stop at any time. Notice, however, that we can only pay for your decisions if you finish the experiment.
